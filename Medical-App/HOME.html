FACILITIES

Primary Care Services

We offer essential healthcare services, including routine check-ups, vaccinations, and the diagnosis and treatment of common illnesses. serves as the first point of contact for patients, addressing general health concerns and providing referrals to specialists when needed.

Diagnostic Services

We provides diagnostic services to identify medical conditions through various means, such as laboratory tests, medical imaging (like X-rays or MRI scans), and physical examinations. These services help healthcare professionals accurately assess a patient's health, leading to informed treatment decisions and improved patient outcomes.

Emergency Care

We offer emergency services to provide immediate medical attention for urgent situations, such as minor injuries, sudden illnesses, and severe symptoms. These services include first aid, rapid assessment, and stabilization of patients, with the option to refer to hospitals for more critical cases.

Physical Therapy and Rehabilitation

Highly trained physical therapists develop personalized treatment plans that include exercises and techniques to improve mobility, reduce pain, and enhance overall physical function, ultimately aiding in the patient's recovery and long-term well-being.

Pharmacy Services

Services include medication counseling, ensuring proper dosages, and monitoring for potential drug interactions or side effects to promote safe and effective medication management for patients.

Home Health Care Services

Our services include general check-ups, management of chronic conditions, vaccinations, and referrals to specialists when necessary, all aimed at maintaining and improving the overall health and well-being of patients.
